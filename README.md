🏎️ F1 Pit Wall — Race Weekend Summarizer
An AI-powered chatbot that gives you a structured summary of every F1 race weekend, broken down by day.

⏱️ Saturday — Qualifying + grid positions

Pick a day tab or use the quick buttons
Ask anything — "Who won the race?", "What happened in qualifying?"
Get an instant, structured recap
